Where does the training data for the AI model come from?
Art. 13 GDPR (direct collection)
Timing: at the moment of collection

Mandatory information:

  • Name and contact details of the controller
  • Purposes and legal basis of the processing
  • Storage period (or the criteria for it)
  • Recipients of the data / third-country transfers
  • Data-subject rights and the right to lodge a complaint

Example: a platform trains its own model on posts its users submit through the service.

Art. 14 GDPR (indirect collection)
Timing: reasonable period (max. 1 month)

Mandatory information:

  • All information required under Art. 13 GDPR
  • IN ADDITION: which categories of data are processed?
  • IN ADDITION: from which source do the data originate?

Example: a model provider scrapes the open web for training data. Because the data are not obtained from the people themselves, the source and the categories must be disclosed, and the practical impossibility exception of Art. 14(5) is read narrowly.
