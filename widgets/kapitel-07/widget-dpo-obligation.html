Decision tree: is a DPO mandatory?

Is the organisation a public authority or public body?
(e.g. a ministry, a public university, a tax office)
Does the core activity consist of regular and systematic monitoring of data subjects on a large scale, OR the large-scale processing of special categories of data (Art. 9 GDPR)?
(e.g. a provider that trains models on large-scale behavioural profiles, or on health or biometric data)
Does national law impose a stricter threshold that the organisation crosses?
(Several Member States add lower headcount thresholds; the GDPR itself sets none for private controllers below the core-activity test.)
