The three-way split (automation vs. augmentation vs. low exposure) follows the task-based method of Gmyrek, Berg & Bescond (2023, ILO), who separate tasks AI could perform outright from tasks where AI assists a human in the loop. The clerical occupation shows the ILO pattern of high exposure concentrated in a female-dominated, higher-income field. Sources: Gmyrek, Berg & Bescond 2023; Acemoglu & Restrepo 2022; Baudchon & Huynh 2025.
