Illustrative model. Per-query energy uses the order-of-magnitude range reported for a large-model query (roughly 0.001–0.01 kWh), about 25–60× a web search. Water intensity is set at roughly 0.5 litres per kWh of consumption, in the range discussed for data-centre cooling and power generation. The rebound assumption (a 10× efficiency gain triggers a 20× rise in demand) is a teaching figure, not a forecast, chosen to make Jevons' paradox visible. Sources: Li et al. 2023; Patterson et al. 2021; Jevons 1866.
